Electronic Housekeeper

What we did

Electronic housekeeper is a system for sales of electronic devices smart home. System provides support at all stages: from ordering and delivery to the user's device setup, technical support. Web-site includes web-shop, customer service area which allows to control the EH device online, technical support forum. CRM application gives a way to control customers' orders, payments, customer accounts, web-shop management, stock-taking functionality, and CMS functions for website.

Key features:

  • Project includes web-site, web-based CRM application and system integration with Electronic Housekeeper devices network.
  • Web-shop management.
  • Content management subsystem.
  • Shopping cart with multiple currencies, delivery price calculation depending on customer's location.
  • Support for PayPal and DIBS payment systems.
  • User area integrated with EH device network.
  • Custom-made support forum.
  • Orders handling, integrated with EH device network. The device activates and sets up after receiving a payment on order.
  • Customer accounts management.
  • Stock taking and planning system.
  • Website is localized in Danish and English.
