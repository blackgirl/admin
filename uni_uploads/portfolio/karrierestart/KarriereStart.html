KarriereStart

What we did

KarriereStart is a leading Norwegian job portal which was made both for people who are searching for a job and companies. It's known more than 80% of jobs are never advertised, it is evident that many job seekers miss the best career opportunities and employers miss the best candidates. KarriereStart helps to prevent these statistics. KarriereStart collects and structures all the information about the job market, industries, occupations and employers throughout Norway. System helps to search for the most suitable candidates for the position, plus it searches jobs that match desires and experience. One of the features of this professional social networking website is extended tools for analysts. Analyze with specified criteria shows the compatibility of the candidate with the vacancy.

Key features:

  • Search vacancies or candidates based numerous criteria such as experience, skills, location, education, previous positions etc.
  • Subscriptions for companies' latest news and regular updates of vacancies allow companies and candidates to keep in touch.
  • In-depth analysis evaluates candidate's suitability for the vacancy.
  • Adding videos to profile from a webcam.
  • Creating complex CV, including videos, photos, presentations etc.
  • Powerful admin panel for companies, which lets to keep in touch with candidates, post latest news, upcoming events, new vacancies etc.
  • Ability to use the portal for companies as a media publisher (company's news feed).
